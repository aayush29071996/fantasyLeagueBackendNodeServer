Fantasy Sports is purely legal in India. It is considered as Game of Skill and is not related to Gambling or betting in any way since users need their knowledge of sports and skills in building up their high performing team.

What is Game of Skill ?

A game of skill is a game where the outcome is determined mainly by mental or physical skill, rather than by chance. A game of skill generally has an element of chance, but skill plays a greater role in determining the outcome.
For more, please refer to the Public Gambling Act,1867 (India) - www.indiankanoon.org/doc/1824663/
Age Limitation : Citizens of india 18 and Above
Anybody who is 18 and above are allowed to play Fantasy sports in India with the exception of Orissa and Assam.Since the laws of these states are unclear with respect to Game of Skill, Residents of Orissa and Assam are allowed to participate only in Play for Free category and not in Play for Cash.
